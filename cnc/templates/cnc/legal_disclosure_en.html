This page provides required disclosure information for online (distance) sales in Japan.

Prices
Tax-inclusive prices are shown on each product/service page. Any shipping or handling fees will be clearly indicated during checkout with the total amount.
Fees other than the product price
Shipping and payment processing fees (if applicable) will be shown at checkout.
Internet connection and communication charges are borne by the customer.
Payment Methods
Credit cards and other methods designated by us (e.g., domestic bank transfer).
Payment Timing
Credit card: processed immediately. / Bank transfer: remit within 3 days of order.
Delivery / Provision Timing
Physical goods: shipped within 3–5 business days after order.
Digital goods / services: available immediately upon payment or upon delivery of an access email.
Returns / Exchanges / Cancellations
Defective items: exchange or refund at our cost (including shipping).
Customer convenience: unopened items may be returned within 10 days after delivery; conditions vary by product/service.
Digital goods / services: due to their nature, refunds are not available once provided, unless otherwise stated on the product page.
Quantity Limits / Application Period (if applicable)
Any limits or deadlines will be stated on the relevant product/service page.
System Requirements (for software)
Supported OS and minimum specs will be stated on each product page.

For reference, see Stripe’s help on the Specified Commercial Transactions Act and how to display a commerce disclosure page.
